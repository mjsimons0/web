About Me

Welcome to my personal website. I'm Michael Simons, an aspiring Computational Mathematician and Data Scientist with a strong foundation in computer science and mathematics. My primary area of interest is in the extraction of meaningful insights from large-scale datasets and application of computational techniques to solve real-world problems.

My academic journey has instilled in me a deep appreciation for the elegance of mathematical theory and its practical applications in technology. I'm particularly fascinated by the intersection of machine learning and software engineering, which I believe holds the key to unlocking innovative solutions across various industries.

I'm constantly learning and expanding my skillset, staying up-to-date with the latest developments in programming, data science and machine learning. As I embark on my professional career, I'm eager to apply my skills to contribute to cutting-edge projects and drive data-informed decision-making.
